Dr Jesica Brown

MB, Bch, B.A.O, MICGP

Dr Jesica Brown graduated from the Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland in 1995. She is a member of the Irish College of General Practice and member of Royal College of Surgeons in Ireland.
She has worked and gained vast experience in general surgical and plastic surgical departments in both Ireland and Northern Ireland. She has also worked in many Dublin hospitals in both medical and accident   emergency departments.
She has a diploma in dermatology from the University of Cardiff. She has special interest in family planning, dermatology and minor surgical procedures.
